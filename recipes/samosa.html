ALOO SAMOSA

DESCRIPTION

A samosa (/səˈmoʊsə/) (listenⓘ) is a fried South Asian[2] pastry with a savory filling that mostly consists of vegetables like spiced potatoes, onions, and peas.

INGREDIENTS

  • MAIDA
  • ALOO
  • MIRCHI PODWER
  • AMCHUR POWDER
  • SALT
  1. MIX WATER AND MAIDA AND MAKE A NICE SOFT DOUGH
  2. BOIL ALOO AND THEN ONCE COOLED MIX AMCHUR,MRICHI POWDER,SALT
  3. MAKE THE SHAPE OF SAMOSA AND STUFF IT WITH ALOO
  4. FRY IT IN GOOD QUALITY OIL AND EAT
